Overview

Richmond Pet Hotel & Spa has been providing professional pet grooming services for animals in the Richmond area for over 10 years. As a certified groomer, we aim to give your pets the best treatment possible and to keep them as happy as possible. What makes us stand out from our competitiors is our partnership with the Pet Philanthropy Circle foundation which aims to enhance the quality of animals lives by promoting greater public awareness of their welfare and the causes that protect them.

This is just one of our luxury spaces we have for your animals to meet other animals and unwind and relax. Our facilities include two football fields of play space, a playground, spa, hair-cuttery, among other amenities.

Excellence

This is what every single one of our employees strive for. They are all pet lovers with years of experience handling animals of all kinds. They have been through substansive training in all aspects of the facility and are happy to take your pet off your hands and treat them to a relaxing getaway weekend!
