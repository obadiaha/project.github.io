About our Personalized Care

 

All members of our staff are trained and experienced with knowledge of pet care and grooming. We strive to offer a deluxe spa experience for both dogs and cats. Custom and specialty cuts and styling are also available.

We use only the finest products that will leave your pet’s coat sparkling fresh and shiny. If you prefer to bring along your own products, we will be happy to use them as well.  Our air-conditioned facilities are kept clean and disinfected at all times.

Your pet’s welfare is our prime concern, and we make sure that they are as comfortable as possible.
