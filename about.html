Who We Are

Here at Richmond Pet Hotel & Spa (RPHS), we strive to give your pet the best treatment imaginable. Our workers treat your pets as if they were their own children. We are all passionate pet lovers here at Richmond Pet Hotel & Spa! We have trained professionals in every field on staff and each person is specialized to their personal job. This ensures that we have the best person for the job in every situation.

Our Partnership

We are not your regular pet hotel and spa! We worked out a special deal with a non-profit organization called Hope for Paws. Their mission is to raise money and awareness for those household pets who suffer and die every year because of negligence and abuse. Every time you buy a product, extend the stay of your pet here at RPHS, or spend any type of money in our gift store or for your animals medical care, 10% of the proeceds are donated to Hope for Paws to further their mission. The staff at Hope for Paws is extremely grateful for your contribution and ensure that the animals affected by their reach are just as grateful as well!
